The Plough is an essential farming equipment used for tilling the soil, breaking it up, and preparing the land for sowing seeds. It increases productivity by making soil aeration and mixing easier.

  • Durability: Rust-resistant and long-lasting

  • Reduces manual labor and increases productivity
